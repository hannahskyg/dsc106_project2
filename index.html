Proposition: Older People are More Likely to Complain Against Police Officers

FOR the Proposition

Design Decisions and Rationale:

  • Used an age cutoffs at 30, data framing at what is considered "older people," aligning the visualization with the proposition being argued.
  • Utilized a bar chart to emphasize a significant difference in CCRB complaints among older and younger groups.
  • Sticking with one color, it keeps attention on bar height differences, emphasizing the persuasive focus on how much older adults complain.

AGAINST the Proposition

Design Decisions and Rationale:

  • Binarizing the age groups to show off the overall data and making the age trend easier to read instantly.
  • Using a line chart illustrates a signfiicant negative slope, emphasizing that younger people make more complaints.
  • Using age bins hide differences in population size with each age group, which can distort the differences in complaints, making the argument more persuasive.
